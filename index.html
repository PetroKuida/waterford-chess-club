A Friendly Hub for Chess Enthusiasts

Join the Waterford Chess Club and become part of a vibrant community dedicated to the game of chess. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, our club offers something for everyone.

About Us

The Waterford Chess Club is committed to fostering a passion for chess in the Waterford community. Our mission is to provide a welcoming environment where players of all levels can learn, compete, and enjoy the game of chess.

Why Choose Us?

Expert coaching and lessons

Join us to benefit from expert coaching and lessons tailored to all skill levels. Our experienced coaches provide personalized guidance and structured training to help you improve, whether you're a beginner or advanced. Gain the skills and confidence needed to reach your full potential.

Regular tournaments and friendly matches

Enjoy regular tournaments and friendly matches that cater to different skill levels. These events offer opportunities to test your abilities, learn from others, and have fun. Stay engaged and challenged with our variety of competitive and recreational events.

A supportive and inclusive community

Become part of our supportive and inclusive community. We foster an environment of camaraderie, respect, and mutual growth. Our diverse and friendly members are always ready to offer support and share their passion for the game, helping you thrive both on and off the field.
